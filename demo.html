Ajax Search Suggest

jQuery.ajax-searchsuggest.js

What's Thisï¼Ÿ
  • jQuery plugin using Google Suggest API to show auto suggestions under the search field.
Keyboard Operation
  • With Tab or ↓ key, you can choose search suggestions from top to bottom.
  • With Shift + Tab or ↑ key, you can choose search suggestions from bottom to top.
  • if you press the Enter key at the suggestion selected, the words enter the search field.
  • When you press the esc key, Search suggestions disappears.
Mouse Operation
  • If you click on the search suggestions, the words enter the search field.
